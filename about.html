About

I'm Alyssa Parten; Florida native, but Alabama living. I'm enjoying life with my husband and two fur babies. My passion is helping those who want to improve their lives and health through exercise, nutrition with sustainable lifestyle changes.

To acheive this, I will develop a program that is not only built for your goals, but your lifestyle as well. Unrealistic dietary changes and training schedules don't work not only because people can't adhere to them, but it defeats them and destroys their confidence. Constant defeat and trying to change your life for a goal that isn't essential to life leads to people giving up on their health and fitness goals. No one has to be a slave to a schedule and diet in order to acheive a dream body or change their health for the better.
